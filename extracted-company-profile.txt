COMPANY OVERVIEW. 
Nolads Engineering Ltd was established in the year 1998 as a Sole Proprietor Business and later 
Incorporated in 2008 as Limited Liability Company. 
During the early years the Company mainly offered services in the Power Generation Sector 
and have been one of the leading specialized services providers in the Supply, Installation and 
Maintenance of Generating Sets in Kenya. We are registered with the Energy and Petroleum 
Regulatory Authority (EPRA) as specialized Electrical Contractors Class A-2. 
Over the years we have broadened our expertise and established a division dealing in Building 
Construction and Civil Engineering Works. We have successfully undertaken several GOK and 
NGO Projects in various areas across the Country. 
Our Head Office is based in the Coastal City of Mombasa – North Coast with a Generating Set 
Parts Outlet in the Mombasa CBD and a Branch Office in Nairobi covering up – Country 
operations.
